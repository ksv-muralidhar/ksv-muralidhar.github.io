Find Similar News

This project finds news articles from a database that are similar to an input news article, using clustering and cosine similarity. During training, the articles are converted into embeddings using SentenceTransformer, embeddings' dimensionality is reduced using PCA, clusters are assigned to news articles. During inference, the news articles in the database belonging to the predicted cluster are filtered, cosine similarity between the input article and the articles in the database (filtered earlier) is computed and the top 'n' similar articles with highest cosine similarity are returned.

  • NLP
